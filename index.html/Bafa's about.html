Bafa's Story

Bafa’s Lomihan in Darangan, Binangonan Rizal offers freshly made meals and a cozy dining experience for everyone.

The Beginning of Bafa's Lomihan

Bafa’s Lomi House opened its doors in 2018 in the heart of Darangan, Binangonan, Rizal. What began as a humble family kitchen slowly grew into a beloved local eatery, known for serving authentic homemade lomi and our signature Bafa’s kikiam that brings comfort to the community.

Our founder, lovingly known as "Bafa", learned the art of cooking lomi from her grandmother. Over the years, she added her own special twist a richer broth, a unique blend of spices, and fresh toppings that make our lomi unforgettable. Every bowl is slow-cooked for hours to achieve its iconic thick, flavorful broth.

As word spread, customers kept coming back for our famous Lomi with Bafa’s Kikiam perfect balance of savory broth, tender noodles, and our crispy homemade kikiam that has become our signature touch.

Today, Bafa’s Lomi House remains committed to offering delicious, affordable, and authentic Filipino comfort food. Each dish is prepared with the same love and dedication we had on our first day. Whether you're dining with family, bonding with friends, or eating solo, we aim to bring warmth, comfort, and happiness in every bowl we serve.
